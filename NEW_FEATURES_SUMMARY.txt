# Hotel Management System - New Features Added

## Overview
I have completed the Hotel Management System frontend by implementing all missing pages and features. The system now provides a comprehensive solution for hotel operations management with proper permission controls and user invitation functionality.

## New Pages Created

### 1. Authentication Pages
- **ForgotPassword.jsx** - Password reset request page with email input
- **ResetPassword.jsx** - Password reset form with token validation
- **SignupInvite.jsx** - Account creation via invitation link

### 2. Category Management
- **Categories.jsx** - Room category listing with grid/table view
- **CategoryForm.jsx** - Create/edit room categories with multilingual support

### 3. Client Management
- **Clients.jsx** - Guest information management with search and filters
- **ClientForm.jsx** - Comprehensive client profile creation/editing with date pickers

### 4. Booking Management
- **Stays.jsx** - Booking and reservation management with status tracking
- **StayForm.jsx** - Create/edit bookings with client and room selection

### 5. Financial Management
- **Invoices.jsx** - Invoice management with payment status tracking
- **InvoiceForm.jsx** - Invoice creation with multiple payment support

### 6. Pricing Management
- **PricePeriodForm.jsx** - Seasonal pricing and special rate management

### 7. Location Management
- **Zones.jsx** & **ZoneForm.jsx** - Zone management within cities
- **Regions.jsx** & **RegionForm.jsx** - Regional management within countries
- **Cities.jsx** & **CityForm.jsx** - City management within regions
- **Countries.jsx** & **CountryForm.jsx** - Country management with codes

### 8. User Management
- **Users.jsx** - User management with role-based access and invitation system

### 9. System Management
- **Permissions.jsx** - Granular permission management interface
